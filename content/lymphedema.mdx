Our Manual Lymphatic Drainage therapy is a gold-standard support for lymphedema (chronic interstitial swelling) management. We treat Secondary Lymphedema due to disruption or damage to the lympphatic system, Primary Lymphedema from congenital or hereditary origin and Idiopathic Lymphedema with no known cause. 

We carefully consider your detailed health and lifestyle history to ensure we create the optimal treatment plan for your needs. Treatment plans are frequently assaessed and readjusted as healing progresses.

In addition to your in office protocol, we also create a complimentary protocol for at-home supportive care, to secure your best therapeutic outcome.
