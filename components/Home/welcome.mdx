# Welcome To Dynamic Lymphatics
___


Certified in the internationally recognized [Casley-Smith](http://www.casleysmithinternational.org/#section4) techniques, we offer the best, most effective decongestive lymphatic therapy in the valley. Sometimes referred to simply as lymphatic massage, decongestive lymphatic therapy is a hands-on treatment that gently aids and directs the flow of lymphatic fluid through the body.

Treatment is highly recommended for:
- maintaining [wellness](/services/wellness), good circulation & immunity
- mitigating chronic swelling of [lymphedema](/services/lymphedema)
- managing [lipidema](/services/lymphedema)
- preparation [before surgery](/services/surgeries)
- aiding recovery [post-surgery](/services/surgeries)
